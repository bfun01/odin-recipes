Italian Panino

Ingredients

  • Bread (Focaccia, Scacciata, Pane de Casa etc)
  • Porchetta (150-200g)
  • Salami (100-150g)
  • Sun-dried tomatoes
  • Rocket
  • Buratta (1x individual tub)
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Slice the bread, drizzle with olive oil and toast.
  2. Slice tomatoes.
  3. (Optional) lightly grill the porchetta

Enjoy!
