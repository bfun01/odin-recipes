Hainan Chicken

Description

One of my favourite recipes, Hainan Chicken is a famous singaporean dish found at local hawker markets.

Ingredients

Chicken Hainan

  • Chicken Maryland (or whole chicken)
  • Spring Onions
  • Ginger
  • Sesame Oil
  • Garlic

Spring Onion and Oil Condiment

  • Spring Onion
  • Peanut Oil
  • Ginger
  • MSG

Chilli Sauce

  • Fresh Chilli
  • Garlic
  • Lemon
  • Sugar

Soy Sauce and Chicken Stock

  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil

Steps

Chicken Hainan

  1. Prepare chicken by removing larger fat deposits. Use a small pan to slowly render the chicken fat.
  2. Season chicken and prepare ginger and garlic for pot.
  3. Add chicken, spring onions, ginger, garlic to a pot and bring to boil.
  4. Remove chicken from pot, wash with cold water. Rub on sesame oil.
  5. Use chicken stock to cook rice.

Spring Onion and Oil Condiment

  1. Slice white part of spring onion, add to metal bowl.
  2. Grate ginger into same bowl.
  3. Add peanut oil into the pot with chicken fat, heat pot until lightly smoking.
  4. Pour oil into the metal bowl containing the spring onion.
  5. Add salt and MSG to taste.

Chilli Sauce

  1. Use a food processor to finely chop chilli, garlic, ginger.
  2. Add lemon, sugar and chicken stock to taste.
  3. Season with salt and MSG to taste.

Soy Sauce and Chicken Stock

  1. Add chicken stock to a bowl.
  2. Add Soy Sauce to taste.
  3. Top with sesame oil.

Enjoy!
