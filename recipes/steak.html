Steak

Description

A simple, yet classic dinner meal. The key to a good steak is the crust. Make sure you follow the steps to make sure you get that maillard reaction.

Ingredients

  • Scotch fillet, thick
  • Rice, Chips or Potato Mash for side
  • Yuzu Kosho (or other condiment) Optional

Steps

  1. Take steak out and cover with paper towels to soak up moisture.
  2. Preheat cast iron skillet on medium-low.
  3. Season liberally with salt, pepper and a light dusting of garlic powder.
  4. Increase cast iron skillet to high heat, wait for the pan to start smoking.
  5. Oil the pan liberally.
  6. Sear the steaks, turning over occasionally until internal temperature reaches 120f.
  7. Rest for 10 minutes and serve.

Enjoy!
